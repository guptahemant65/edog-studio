Confirmation Patterns

Every destructive or irreversible action needs proper confirmation UX

27a · Inline Confirmation

Button transforms on click instead of opening a dialog. Click once to expand, then Confirm or Cancel. Auto-reverts after 5 seconds.

Re-deploy
Re-deploy will restart the service. 5
Cancel DAG
Cancel will stop all running DAG nodes. 5
Force Unlock
Force unlock may cause data corruption. 5
27b · Destructive Confirmation Dialog

Centered overlay for high-impact actions. Type the workspace name to enable the Delete button.

Delete Workspace 'FMLVWS'?

This will permanently delete the workspace and all 6 items inside it. This action cannot be undone.

27c · Undo Pattern

After non-destructive actions, offer undo instead of pre-confirmation. Click Undo within 5 seconds to revert.
